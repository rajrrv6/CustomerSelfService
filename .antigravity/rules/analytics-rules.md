# Analytics & QA Scoring Rules

## 1. Tenant Telemetry Realism
- Expose clear business metrics (CSAT scores, response rates, NPS distributions, live conversation counts, and containment percentages).
- Keep numerical data formatted according to locale (EN/AR).

## 2. Customer Sentiment Visuals
- Group sentiment driver analysis into color-coded category cards (positive, neutral, negative highlights) to display customer feedback drivers (billing, delivery delays) clearly.
- Render recent surveys in structured lists with satisfaction values, agent assignments, and customer text logs.

## 3. QA Scorecard Boundaries
- Ensure the QA Manager persona has access to Customer Satisfaction (CSAT/NPS) dashboard views, maintaining role visibility checks.
