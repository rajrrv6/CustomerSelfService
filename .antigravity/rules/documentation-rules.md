# Documentation & Process Rules

## 1. Sprint logs & Checkpoints updates
- Document completed frontend features inside `docs/sprints/` under structured sprint logs mapping scope, outcomes, and decisions.
- Log manual layout verifications and remaining carryovers under `docs/checkpoints/`.

## 2. Implementation Plans & Decisions
- Maintain detailed plans (`docs/plans/`) outlining proposed architectures, files affected, and risks prior to executing complex features.
- Log significant architectural design choices under ADR format (`docs/decisions/`), specifying contexts, decisions, and consequences.
